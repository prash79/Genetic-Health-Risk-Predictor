About Ayushman Bharat PM-JAY

Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) is the largest health assurance scheme in the world, aiming to provide a health cover of Rs. 5 lakhs per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization to over 12 crore poor and vulnerable families.

Ground workers like **ASHA (Accredited Social Health Activists)** serve as the backbone of healthcare delivery, interfacing directly with families. This tool assists ASHA workers in predicting and registering hereditary risks for chronic diseases so that preventive actions and diagnostic referrals can be initiated early.

About the Family Health Risk Predictor

The Family Health Risk Predictor uses 7 disease-specific machine learning models to analyze a beneficiary's hereditary background, current lifestyle, symptoms, and basic vitals (systolic/diastolic BP, blood sugar, BMI). By calculating quantitative risk percentages, it helps classify patients into **Low, Medium, and High risk** categories, providing clear pathways to nearest community health facilities.
